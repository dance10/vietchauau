## Overview

BaiKiemTraVEU là nền tảng luyện thi trực tuyến cho **Anh Ngữ Việt Châu Âu (VEU)**, giúp học viên luyện tập các kỳ thi Cambridge Starters/Movers/Flyers, KET, PET, IELTS, TOEIC và Tuyển Sinh lớp 10. Thiết kế hướng đến cả học sinh và phụ huynh — kết hợp giữa sự tin cậy của thương hiệu giáo dục lâu năm (navy-deep + gold) với cảm giác hiện đại, thân thiện (surface sáng, card mềm mại, animation tinh tế).

Trang đích (HomePage) mở đầu với hero gradient navy-xanh sử dụng dot-grid và glowing orb làm hiệu ứng nền, kèm stat cards trong suốt. Phần exam section bên dưới dùng card trượt ngang (horizontal scroll) với viền trên màu theo từng cấp độ. Leaderboard dùng podium + table với filter tabs và time-filter pills.

Trang thi (ExamPage) có bố cục tập trung: timer-progress-question-result flow, với progress dots dạng ô vuông (rounded-lg), question card dạng thẻ trắng, và option buttons có trạng thái selected rõ ràng.

**Key Characteristics:**
- **Navy-deep + gold** là bộ đôi thương hiệu — xanh navy làm nền tảng cho hero, footer, và primary actions; vàng gold làm điểm nhấn cho logo, stat numbers, và trophy.
- **Amber CTA** (`#f59e0b`) dành riêng cho call-to-action chính (button "Bắt đầu ngay") — một màu cam rực rỡ tạo contrast mạnh trên nền xanh.
- **Card với viền trên màu** (colored top border) — mỗi cấp độ thi có màu riêng (blue cho young, purple cho IELTS, cyan cho TOEIC, green cho tuyển sinh), thể hiện qua border-top 3px trên exam card.
- **Horizontal scroll exam section** — danh sách bài thi được bố trí dạng cuộn ngang với nút điều hướng trái/phải, tối ưu cho mobile.
- **Bộ surface container** theo Material Design — 5 cấp độ surface (dim, bright, container-low, container, container-high, container-highest) cho phép phân tầng depth tinh tế không cần shadow.
- **Dark mode đầy đủ** — toàn bộ surface colors đảo ngược qua `.dark` class, lưu preference trong localStorage.
- **Hiệu ứng hover scale + translate** — button và card luôn có `hover:scale-105 hover:-translate-y-0.5` cho cảm giác phản hồi vật lý nhẹ nhàng.
- **Animation scroll reveal** — section dùng Intersection Observer để fade-up khi scroll vào viewport.
- **Tabular numbers cho timer** — `font-feature-settings: "tnum"` để số đồng hồ không bị giật khi thay đổi.

## Colors

> **Source:** `src/index.css` theme variables, `src/config/constants.js` level colors.

### Brand & Accent
- **Primary** (`{colors.primary}` — `#003399`): Màu chủ đạo của brand. Dùng cho button chính, nav active state, timer bar, link emphasis, và làm màu nền cho toast success.
- **Navy Deep** (`{colors.navy-deep}` — `#001a33`): Màu tối nhất trong gradient hero. Dùng làm footer background, mobile drawer background.
- **Navy Light** (`{colors.navy-light}` — `#002a4d`): Màu trung gian trong hero gradient.
- **Blue Light** (`{colors.blue-light}` — `#1a5cbb`): Màu xanh sáng trong hero gradient; cũng là hover state của primary button.
- **Gold** (`{colors.gold}` — `#c8a84e`): Màu vàng điểm nhấn. Dùng cho logo khi ở hero, stat numbers, trophy icon, rank #1 badge và border.
- **Gold Light** (`{colors.gold-light}` — `#d4b86a`): Hover variant của gold button.
- **CTA** (`{colors.cta}` — `#f59e0b`): Amber cam — màu CTA duy nhất. Dùng cho button "Bắt đầu ngay", "Nộp bài", và các conversion action chính.
- **CTA Hover** (`{colors.cta-hover}` — `#d97706`): Darken của CTA cho hover state.

### Surface
- **Surface** (`{colors.surface}` — `#ffffff`): Default surface cho cards, components. Trong exam: question card, result card, timer.
- **BG Soft** (`{colors.bg-soft}` — `#f8fafc`): Màu nền của các section alternate (khi không dùng surface). Dùng làm section band màu sáng.
- **Border Light** (`{colors.border-light}` — `#e2e8f0`): Border 1px cho cards, tables, inputs, dividers.

## Typography

### Font Family
Toàn bộ hệ thống dùng **Inter** (loaded từ Google Fonts) ở các weight từ 400 (normal) đến 900 (black). Font fallback: `ui-sans-serif, system-ui, sans-serif`.

Inter at weight 400 is clean and readable for body; weight 700 (bold) carries card titles and buttons; weight 900 (black) carries hero headlines, score numbers, and the logo brandmark — the heaviest weight is part of the brand's confident educational voice.

### Principles
- **Weight 900 is the brand signature.** Hero headlines, score, stat numbers, logo, and timer all use Inter weight 900. The brand reads as confident and bold because of it.
- **Section titles are uppercase + tracking.** Every section eyebrow uses `display-section` (12px, weight 900, all-caps, 0.2em tracking) — this is the brand's structural voice.
- **Badges are all-caps + wider tracking.** `badge` token at 10px, weight 700, 0.1em tracking. Used for exam level badges and inline metadata.
- **Timer uses tabular figures.** The `tnum` feature ensures numbers don't shift width as they change — critical for the countdown timer's visual stability.
- **Body is always weight 400.** Body text never uses bold (reserved for headings and buttons).

### Note on Font
Inter is open-source via Google Fonts at `https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter`. Load weights 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900. Default system font stack (`ui-sans-serif, system-ui, sans-serif`) is an acceptable fallback but loses the brand's Inter-specific character.

## Layout

### Spacing System
- **Base unit**: 4px. Every spacing value is a multiple of 4.
- **Tokens**: `{spacing.xxs}` 4px · `{spacing.xs}` 8px · `{spacing.sm}` 12px · `{spacing.md}` 16px · `{spacing.lg}` 20px · `{spacing.xl}` 24px · `{spacing.xxl}` 32px · `{spacing.3xl}` 48px · `{spacing.4xl}` 64px · `{spacing.5xl}` 96px · `{spacing.card-padding}` 20px · `{spacing.card-gap}` 16px.
- **Section padding**: `{spacing.3xl}` (48px) top/bottom on mobile, `{spacing.4xl}` (64px) on desktop within the `max-w-7xl` container.
- **Card internal padding**: `{spacing.card-padding}` 20px on exam cards; `{spacing.lg}` 20px to `{spacing.xl}` 24px on question/result cards.
- **Inline gap**: buttons and icon rows use `{spacing.sm}` 12px to `{spacing.md}` 16px between siblings.

### Grid & Container
- **Max-width**: `max-w-7xl` (1280px) for both HomePage sections and ExamPage content. Content centers with `mx-auto` and horizontal gutters of `{spacing.lg}` 16px on mobile, `{spacing.xl}` 24px on desktop.
- **Column patterns on HomePage**:
  - Hero stat grid: 3-up (`grid-cols-1 sm:grid-cols-3`) at desktop, stacked at mobile.
  - Exam card row: horizontal scroll container (`flex overflow-x-auto`) with cards of fixed width (`w-[240px] sm:w-[260px]`).
  - Leaderboard podium: 3-card flex row centered, with the #1 card elevated (`-translate-y-4 md:-translate-y-6`) and wider.
  - Leaderboard table: single-column vertical list with header row on desktop.
- **ExamPage layout**: single-column centered (`max-w-4xl`), header row (title + timer), progress dots row (horizontal scroll on mobile), question card, bottom nav (prev/next buttons).

### Whitespace Philosophy
Các section trên HomePage sử dụng background alternating (`bg-surface` → `bg-bg-soft`) để tạo nhịp điệu thị giác — người dùng cảm nhận được sự chuyển đổi giữa các khối nội dung mà không cần divider.

Section spacing rộng (`py-12 md:py-16` ≈ 48–64px) cho khoảng thở giữa các exam band. Hero có padding lớn hơn (`pt-20 pb-16 md:pt-24 md:pb-24`) để gradient nền có không gian lan tỏa.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Reserve `{colors.primary}` for primary buttons, nav active states, timer primary, and the primary CTA in exam cards. It should be the most frequent action color.
- Use `{colors.cta}` (amber) exclusively for the hero "Bắt đầu ngay" button and the exam "Nộp bài" button — it's the brand's high-energy conversion color.
- Apply `{colors.gold}` to the logo when on dark backgrounds, stat numbers, trophy/medal elements, and rank #1 indicators.
- Set every section eyebrow in `{typography.display-section}` (12px/900/uppercase/0.2em tracking) — it's the brand's consistent section voice.
- Use badge at 10px/700/0.1em tracking for all exam level badges and metadata pills.
- Apply the colored top-border on exam cards — the 3px `border-t-{level}` is the card's identity signature.
- Use alternating section backgrounds (`bg-surface` / `bg-bg-soft`) to create visual rhythm between exam sections.
- Enable `font-feature-settings: "tnum"` on the timer countdown to prevent number width jitter.
- Apply `hover:scale-105 hover:-translate-y-0.5` to all interactive elements (buttons, cards) — it's the brand's tactile feedback signature.
- Use surface-container colors for depth separation rather than heavy shadows.

### Don't
- Don't use `{colors.cta}` (amber) for non-CTA elements — it's reserved for conversion actions only.
- Don't apply `{colors.gold}` to body text; it's an accent color limited to stat numbers, logo, and trophy elements.
- Don't use weight 900 for body paragraphs — 900 is for headlines, scores, logo, and timer only.
- Don't remove the 3px colored top border from exam cards — it's the primary visual differentiator between exam levels.
- Don't use uppercase for anything other than section eyebrows and badges; headings are sentence-case.
- Don't make buttons smaller than 32px touch target height (WCAG requirement).
- Don't disable dark mode support — transitions between light/dark must be smooth via `transition-colors duration-300`.
- Don't use shadows heavier than the level 2 CTA shadow on non-CTA elements; the brand prefers surface container colors over heavy drop shadows.
- Don't change the hero gradient direction or color stops without maintaining the navy-deep → navy-light → blue-light progression.

  - Icon buttons: `p-2` (32×32px minimum) within a `h-14 md:h-16` nav bar.
  - Filter pills: `px-4 py-1.5` (24px height) with adequate spacing between items.
- Form inputs (future): minimum 40px height.

### Collapsing Strategy
- **Hero section**: headline drops from 48px to 30px on mobile; stat grid from 3-up to 1-up; CTA buttons from horizontal to vertical stack.
- **Navigation**: full link row + icon buttons at `md:` breakpoint; collapses to logo + hamburger + dark mode toggle on mobile. Drawer opens as slide-in overlay at 72vw width.
- **Exam sections**: horizontal scroll container with `hide-scrollbar`; scroll-arrow buttons visible only at `md:` and above.
- **Leaderboard**: podium 3-card stays horizontal at all breakpoints (flex shrink); exam detail column in table hidden below `lg:` breakpoint.
- **Question page**: question text drops from `text-xl` to `text-lg`; card padding tightens from `p-8` to `p-6`.
- **Result page**: retry/home buttons from horizontal flex to vertical stack on mobile.

### Animation Behavior
- **Hero animations**: `animate-fade-up` staggered (0/100/200/300/400ms delays) on hero elements. Each element fades up 24px over 0.7s.
- **Scroll reveal**: sections use `reveal` class (opacity 0, translateY 24px) which flips to `revealed` via Intersection Observer when scrolling into viewport.
- **Card hover**: `transition-all duration-300 ease-out` for smooth hover lift.
- **Button hover**: `transition-all duration-200` — faster than cards, for snappy feedback.
- **Nav transition**: `transition-all duration-300` for background/color/text changes on scroll.
- **Toast**: `slide-up` animation (100px translateY → 0) over 0.3s.
- **Trophy**: `trophy-bounce` animation (Y oscillation) at 2.5s interval, infinite on podium section heading.

### Dark Mode
- Toggle via Sun/Moon icon button in nav.
- Transition: `transition-colors duration-300` on the main layout container.
- Persisted in `localStorage("theme")`.
- System preference detected via `prefers-color-scheme: dark` media query on first load.

### Building Pages

1. **Start with the hero section**: Use `hero-band` with gradient backdrop, dot-grid overlay, and two glowing orbs. Headline uses `display-hero` (48px → 30px mobile), gold emphasis on key word(s), CTA button pair (`button-cta-lg` + `button-cta-secondary`).

2. **Add exam sections**: Each section gets a `SectionTitle` (eyebrow + underline), then a horizontal scroll row of `card-exam` components. Alternate section backgrounds: `bg-surface` for even, `bg-bg-soft` for odd.

3. **Add leaderboard section**: Podium (3 `card-podium` components centered, #1 elevated) + table container with tab bar and filter pills.

4. **Exam page**: `max-w-4xl mx-auto px-4 py-6 md:py-8 flex flex-col gap-6` — header row (title + timer), progress dots, question card, bottom navigation.

5. **Apply dark mode**: All surfaces get `dark:` variants. Use `.dark` class on `<html>` to trigger CSS variable overrides defined in `index.css`.
